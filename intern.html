Internship Opportunities

If you are between the ages 14-17 and would like to get a small taste of the research experience, please consider applying for an internship role through this form as we always have more work that someone could do.

Please only apply for the role if you have significant experience with the languages Python and c++ and have a strong fundamental understanding of MDPs and RL theory. If you make it past the initial screening, we will get back to you and contact you for an interview.
